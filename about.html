About Me

I'm Slendy (SlendermanHG).

This page is just me talking to you directly. I live in the USA, work a full-time job, and build this project during focused off-hours. My brain is very ADHD/hyperfocus, and this became the place where that energy turns into useful things.

10+ years building SLendy is my nickname Still maintaining older builds Always building something new

Why slendystuff exists

The name came from years of being called Slendy and using SlendermanHG online. The reason behind it is simple: I got tired of watching good people lose hours to confusing tech. I wanted a space where I could fix that in a practical, human way.

"Happy, relief, ease. That's what I want people to feel."
