About

Built by a real person for people who think fast

Slendy Stuff exists for founders, creators, and operators with big ideas, half-finished systems, and zero patience for confusing tech talk.

What I actually do

  • I build software tools that solve specific workflow pain points.
  • I design automation and bots for repeatable daily operations.
  • I provide support, cleanup, and managed tuning after launch.
  • I take rough ideas and turn them into scoped, testable builds.

"I care more about what works on your worst day than what looks perfect in a demo."

- Slendy Stuff

How I build

  • Speed with control: move quickly without creating fragile systems.
  • Clarity over complexity: plain language, visible scope, no guessing.
  • Iterate from reality: decisions are based on usage, not assumptions.
  • Stay available: support remains open after delivery.

What working together feels like

1. You bring the raw idea

Voice notes, bullet points, screenshots, and half-formed thoughts are all valid starting inputs.

2. I shape it into a build map

You get concrete steps, clear priorities, and a practical sequence that avoids overwhelm.

3. We ship and support

Delivery is not the end. You keep access to fixes, guidance, and maintenance paths.
