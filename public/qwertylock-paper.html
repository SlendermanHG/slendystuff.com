How to Use Safely

  • Share the phrase once by voice, in person, or a different channel from the message link.
  • Never text the shared phrase in the same SMS/MMS thread as the private link.
  • Use Phrase QR only face-to-face. Do not screenshot or forward it.
  • Anyone with both the link and phrase can decode the message until the selected open limit or expiry is reached.
  • Speech features may use browser or device speech services. Skip them for maximum privacy.
  • This is a privacy tool, not a promise against compromised phones, screenshots, malware, or someone watching the screen.
