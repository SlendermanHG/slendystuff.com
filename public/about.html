About Me

Technology has never been a side interest. It has always had a hold.

SlendyStuff is built around a person who has been pulled toward technology since childhood, someone who can lose days inside a problem because the process of figuring it out feels almost magnetic.

Origin

Where this comes from

There is no manufactured founder myth here. The core truth is simpler and stronger: technology has held attention from the earliest years. Some people enjoy it. Some tolerate it. For SlendyStuff, it has always been the kind of fascination that changes the speed of time.

Golf can be enjoyable. Life can be enjoyable. But technology has the strange ability to collapse hours into seconds. The right problem, the right machine, the right puzzle, and suddenly the night is gone.

Why it matters

The site is personal because the work is personal

This is not positioned like a faceless tech brand because the work has always happened in human relationships: helping family, helping friends, helping their friends, helping churches, helping small businesses, helping one-shot creators, and helping strangers when the situation called for it.

SlendyStuff is the organized home for that instinct.

Turning points

No grand launch story. Just a real opening.

The major shift is not that the fascination suddenly appeared. It is that there is finally room to treat that fascination as more than a background passion and let it become something people can directly experience.

Stage one

Technology as default gravity

Curiosity toward devices, systems, and problem solving did not need to be forced. It was already there, pulling attention in every direction at once.

Stage two

Helping whoever was nearby

The work spread naturally across family, friends, communities, churches, and creators. Not because it was packaged, but because people needed help and the help was there.

Stage three

Building a place where the gifts can grow

SlendyStuff exists so those abilities can stop living as scattered favors and start living as a visible, useful, evolving body of work.

Capabilities

Wide interest. Uneven depth. Real breadth.

The skill set is intentionally broad because the curiosity is broad. SlendyStuff is not built around one frozen niche. The pattern is fascination, discovery, overlap, and practical use.

  • Website setup, repair, and design
  • Discord bot creation and customization
  • AI integration where it actually helps
  • Computer repair and building
  • Remote troubleshooting and guided support
  • Maintaining websites, domains, and email systems
Personality

Professional enough to trust. Strange enough to remember.

The user direction for the brand was clear: the design should let the scatterbrained quality show. Not as a mistake, but as texture. The personality should live on the border between serious and clownish.

That means the site does not try to hide the human being behind the work. It lets the weird wiring stay visible.
