How pricing works

Extra work is billed clearly.

The standard additional work rate is $95/hour. Major redesigns and new features are quoted separately unless they are already included in an existing plan.

Vendor costs may include hosting, domain, DNS, email, storage, database, and transactional email services.

Common service models

Choose the ownership model that fits.

  • Build plus handoff for clients who want ownership and lower long-term dependence
  • Build plus managed service for non-technical clients who want ongoing help
  • Managed hosting with optional handoff for convenience now and transfer later
